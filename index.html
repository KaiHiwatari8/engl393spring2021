Create your own Reaction Role Discord Bot

A tutorial to create to own custom-branded multipurpose Discord bot and host it for free.

Introduction

If you manage or own a Discord guild with numerous members, you probably use bots to automate common functions such as custom welcome message, reaction roles, YouTube notifications, and much more. Even if you have enough funds, you would probably not want to spend them on having custom bots built for your guild. So, use existing bots? You can but you do not have to unless you do not have programming background. Nevertheless, you can learn to program for free on YouTube anytime. FreeCodeCamp is what I would personally recommend.

Wondering why you should learn to create a Discord bot?

Custom Bot Branding

You can get rid of third-party bots that need you to vote for them to keep using their bot.

All features in just one bot

Instead of managing tons of bots, keep is simple and multi-functioning with just one bot.

Personal Project

Show it off as your personal projects on resume or LinkedIn to demonstrate your skills.

Freelance

Monetize your skills by creating custom bots for people who cannot code on Fiverr, UpWork, Freelancer, etc.

Keep Practicing

Coding is improved only when you keep practicing to write code and solve problems.

Keep Learning

Keep your brain active by learning a new framework.
